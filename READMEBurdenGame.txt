This is the documentation for my current ongoing game, A Hero's Burden
It is a text-based narrative game, with the main 'burden' mechanic that the hero accumualtes based on their violent actions. I am making from scratch so I can understand better how logic and data manipulation works in Python, while making something that has entertainment value.
In the game's code, I have numerous functions that control the flow of the game. I have made functions act as different 'scenes' for the game, for example gameStart(), firstChoice() and secondChoice()
The game could be considered complete as in creating the logic and flow, only an ending and narration is not written yet.
